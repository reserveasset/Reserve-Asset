Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reserve Asset?

Reserve Asset is a decentralized digital wealth token system based on real-world elements and rare minerals, combining limited supply with scientifically verified scarcity.

How can I pay with cryptocurrencies?

Select your preferred cryptocurrency (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Pi, Stellar, XRP, USDT, USDC) and blockchain in the payment section. Follow the instructions to send funds via your wallet to the provided address.

Which blockchains are supported for payments?

We support payments on Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Pi Network, Stellar, XRP Ledger, and TRON, depending on the cryptocurrency chosen (e.g., USDT on Ethereum, Solana, or TRON).

Is the supply limited?

Absolutely. Each Reserve Asset is assigned a fixed maximum supply, creating true scarcity inspired by physical constraints of Earth's periodic elements.
